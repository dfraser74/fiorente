Our Founder & Managing Director

Ash prides himself on being recognised by his clients & peers as an experienced financial advisor.

With over 25 years of experience as a financial adviser, Ash has been recognised as a leader in his profession, having won multiple awards. His high technical expertise and unique ability to communicate with clients (making the complex simple) are why he has attracted and retained loyal clients over his many years in the industry.

As a partner of a company with more than 150 employees, Ash became increasingly aware of losing connection with his clients. Taking pride in sharing his knowledge and commitment to providing exceptional client experiences, Ash committed himself to serving with a more personalised approach, leading to the establishment of Fiorente Advisory with office hubs in Melbourne, the Sunshine Coast, and Townsville.

What is the significance of the word “Fiorente”? It is an Italian adjective for “flourishing and thriving”. This simple word describes my desire for my company as well as the financial position of my clients.

“My focus as a Financial Advisor is educating and assisting clients to make the best decisions in building and protecting their wealth to ensure they have the income they desire to live their best life.”

If you would like to understand the benefits of having an experienced Financial Advisor provide you with financial peace of mind, contact Ash today for a consultation.

Tim Renouf

Tim has had over 10 years working as a financial adviser and has a proven track record of looking after his clients with excellent service and a professional approach. He has a Bachelor of Commerce (Accounting) and an Advanced Diploma (Financial Planning).

In 2022, he joined the Fiorente Advisory team as a Senior Financial Adviser, having previously worked in the Big 4 banks, a stockbroking firm, and a private practice. Tim prides himself on explaining strategies in a way that is easy to understand and, as a result, builds a strong rapport with his clients.

Away from the office, Tim loves spending time with his young family and surfing at Alexandra Headland.

Jodie Maguire

With a solid foundation in Corporate Accounting and a passion for helping others, Jodie made the leap into financial planning in 2012 after taking time out to raise her young family. Since becoming a registered adviser in 2016, she has built a reputation for delivering clear, practical advice that empowers her clients to make confident financial decisions.

Jodie takes pride in forming genuine, long-lasting relationships and has guided individuals and families through all stages of life, from building wealth to navigating major life transitions. Her approachable nature and ability to simplify even the most complex financial concepts make her a trusted adviser and steady hand during pivotal moments.

When she’s not working with clients, Jodie embraces the laid-back Sunshine Coast lifestyle. She enjoys spending time with her family and staying active with her much-loved border collie.
