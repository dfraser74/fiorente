Our Founder

Ash prides himself in being recognised by his clients & peers as an experienced financial advisor.

With over 20 years as a financial adviser, Ash has been recognised as a leader in his profession, having won multiple awards as a financial adviser. His high technical expertise and unique ability to communicate with clients (making the complex simple) are why he has attracted and retained loyal clients over his many years in the industry.

As a partner of a company with more than 150 employees, Ash became increasingly aware of losing connection with his clients. Taking pride in sharing his knowledge and commitment to providing exceptional client experiences, Ash committed himself to serving with a more personalised approach, leading to the start of Fiorente Advisory with office hubs in Melbourne, on the Sunshine Coast and Townsville.

What is the significance of the word “Fiorente”? It is an Italian adjective for “flourishing and thriving”. This simple word describes my desire for my company as well as the financial position of my clients.

“My focus as a Financial Advisor is educating and assisting clients to make the best decisions in building and protecting their wealth to ensure they have the income they desire to live their best life.”

If you would like to understand the benefits of having an experienced Financial Advisor provide you with financial peace of mind, contact Ash today for a consultation

Tim Renouf

Tim has had over 10 years working as a financial adviser and has a proven track record of looking after his clients with excellent service and a professional approach. He has a Bachelor of Commerce (Accounting) and an Advanced Diploma (Financial Planning).

Tracy Smith

Tracy holds an Advanced Diploma in Financial Services and has been in the financial planning industry for 23 years. Tracy started her financial planning career as a Financial Planner helping clients implement strategies and plans to achieve their personal financial goals, whether they be building wealth, protecting their family and assets, or planning for retirement. During this time, she enjoyed building strong relationships with her clients and being able to provide solutions to people’s financial concerns.

After having a family, Tracy moved into a Paraplanning role where she was able to use her extensive financial planning skills to support her colleagues in developing strategy, writing advice documents, completing product research, and overseeing compliance and process requirements. Tracy found that she loved the support role, being able to use her analytical and technical skills to support the advice process to ensure customers received quality advice.

Tracy is part of the team at Fiorente Advisory that supports our advisers in preparing and implementing client advice and providing ongoing support to our valued customers.

Tracy moved to the Sunshine Coast in 2019 from Central Qld and is enjoying being close to the beach and all that the Sunny Coast has to offer. When not working, Tracy can be found walking her dog, reading, hanging out with family and friends over a coffee or tending to her garden.
